Important Notes for South Asian Women:

  • South Asian women often experience menopause earlier (around age 46-47) than the global average.
  • You may experience different symptoms, particularly anxiety, palpitations, and severe fatigue.
  • Don't hesitate to discuss cultural concerns or preferences with your doctor.
  • HRT is safe and effective for most women - don't let stigma prevent you from seeking treatment.
